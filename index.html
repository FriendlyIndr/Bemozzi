Frequently Asked
Questions

How do I place an order?

Simply browse our catalogue, pick your style and size, then tap "Order via WhatsApp". We'll confirm your order and share payment details over chat — no app needed.

What sizes do you offer?

We carry XS through XXL across all styles. Each product page lists exact measurements. If you're between sizes, we recommend sizing up for a relaxed fit or down for a trim look.

How long does delivery take?

Orders placed before 2 PM are dispatched the same day within Pune. Pan-India delivery typically takes 3–5 working days. You'll receive a tracking link once your order ships.

Can I get a custom t-shirt?

Absolutely. We do DTF printing, screen printing, and embroidery. Reach us on WhatsApp with your design and we'll give you a quote within a few hours.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 7 days of delivery for unworn, unwashed items with original tags. Custom orders are non-returnable. Contact us on WhatsApp to initiate a return.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept UPI, bank transfer, and cash on delivery for select pincodes. Payment details are shared after you confirm your order over WhatsApp.

About
Bemozzi.

We're a small team out of Pune that believes a great t-shirt shouldn't cost a fortune. Every piece we make is cut, sewn, and quality-checked in-house — no middlemen, no markups you don't deserve.

It started with a simple frustration: good-looking basics that felt terrible to wear, or ones that felt great but looked terrible. We decided to fix that. We source certified cotton, work with skilled craftspeople, and obsess over the details most brands overlook — stitching, collar shape, weight, fit.

Today we ship across India, take custom orders on WhatsApp, and still reply to every message ourselves. That part isn't changing.
